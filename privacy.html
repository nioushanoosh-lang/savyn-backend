Privacy Policy

How Savyn collects, uses, and protects your financial data

Effective Date: March 24, 2026 · Last Updated: March 24, 2026

At Savyn, your privacy is our highest priority. We built this app to help you save money and pay off debt faster — not to sell your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We will never sell your personal or financial information to third parties.

1
Information We Collect

Account Information — When you create a Savyn account, we collect your email address and the password you create (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text).

Financial Data via Plaid — When you connect your bank accounts, loans, or credit cards, we use Plaid (a third-party financial data provider) to securely retrieve:

  • Account balances and account names
  • Transaction history (to calculate spending patterns and upcoming bills)
  • Loan balances, interest rates, and minimum payments
  • Credit card balances and APR information
🔒 We never store your bank login credentials. Plaid handles authentication using OAuth — your username and password go directly to your bank, never to Savyn.

Usage Data — We collect basic app usage information such as which features you use and how often, to improve the product. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

2
How We Use Your Information

We use your financial data for one purpose: to provide you with personalized debt payoff recommendations and savings insights.

  • Calculate your disposable income after bills and safety buffer
  • Identify your highest-cost debt using the avalanche method
  • Generate daily recommendations on how much extra to pay
  • Project interest savings and updated payoff dates
  • Power the Savyn AI advisor with your account context
  • Track your progress and celebrate milestones

We do not use your data for advertising, credit underwriting, marketing targeting, or any purpose beyond delivering the Savyn service to you.

3
How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Plaid — To connect your financial accounts. Plaid's privacy policy governs their use of your data at plaid.com/legal
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Your financial summary is sent to Claude AI to power the advisor chat feature. No raw account numbers or credentials are shared
  • Service providers — Hosting, analytics, and infrastructure providers who are contractually bound to protect your data
  • Legal requirements — If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users
4
Data Security

We take the security of your financial data seriously and implement industry-standard protections:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • We never store raw bank credentials, card numbers, or account passwords
  • Plaid access tokens are stored securely and never exposed to the client
  • Our servers are hosted on Railway with encrypted storage
  • We conduct regular security reviews of our codebase and infrastructure
While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong unique password for your Savyn account and enable two-factor authentication when available.
5
Your Rights (CCPA — California Residents)

As a California-based company, we comply fully with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Access a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Delete your personal data from our systems
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@savynapp.com and we will respond within 45 days.

6
Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete all your personal and financial data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

You can request account deletion at any time by emailing privacy@savynapp.com or through the Profile settings in the app.

7
Children's Privacy

Savyn is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@savynapp.com and we will delete it promptly.

8
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify you via email or in-app notification if the changes are material.

Your continued use of Savyn after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We're committed to transparency. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, we'll respond within 48 hours.
